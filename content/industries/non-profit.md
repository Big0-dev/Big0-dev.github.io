## Case Study Highlight

### Civic Engagement Platform
A grassroots civic organization came to us with 150,000 database records—96% of which were spam, duplicates, or invalid entries. Their legacy system provided no analytics, and traditional cloud solutions were financially out of reach.

We delivered a complete digital transformation: comprehensive data cleanup yielding 6,500+ verified members, a real-time analytics dashboard, bilingual website with RTL support, WhatsApp-based verification, tiered user management for staff operations, and resilient deployment on shared hosting—achieving 80% cost savings compared to traditional cloud infrastructure.
