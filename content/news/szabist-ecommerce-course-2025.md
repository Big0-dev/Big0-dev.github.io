Hassan Kamran, founder of Big0, is excited to announce a strategic academic partnership with [SZABIST University](https://szabist-isb.edu.pk/) to deliver a comprehensive, hands-on E-Commerce course tailored specifically for 7th semester Accounting and Finance students.

## Program Overview

This 3 credit hour graded course bridges the gap between traditional finance education and modern digital commerce, equipping future finance professionals with practical e-commerce skills essential for today's digital economy. Students will gain real-world experience in building, managing, and analyzing e-commerce operations from a financial perspective.

### Course Details

- **Credits**: 3 Credit Hours (Graded)
- **Target Audience**: 7th Semester Accounting & Finance Students
- **Format**: Hands-on, Project-Based Learning
- **University**: [SZABIST University](https://szabist-isb.edu.pk/)
- **Instructor**: Hassan Kamran, Founder & CEO, Big0

## What This Means for Students

This collaboration represents a significant milestone in modernizing finance education in Pakistan. While traditional accounting and finance curricula focus on financial statements and analysis, this course prepares students for the digital transformation happening across industries.

Students will learn:
- E-commerce platform development and management
- Digital payment systems and financial integration
- Online business models and revenue streams
- E-commerce analytics and financial reporting
- Digital marketing fundamentals for commerce
- Supply chain and inventory management systems
- Compliance and regulatory considerations

## About the Partnership

SZABIST University has consistently demonstrated commitment to providing industry-relevant education. This partnership aligns with both institutions' vision of preparing students for the rapidly evolving digital economy.

Hassan Kamran brings over a decade of experience building e-commerce solutions and digital platforms through Big0, having worked with clients across finance, retail, healthcare, and technology sectors. This real-world expertise translates directly into practical, career-ready skills for students.

The course curriculum draws from Big0's extensive work in e-commerce development and web application development, ensuring students learn current industry practices and technologies.

## Hands-On Learning Approach

Unlike traditional lecture-based courses, this program emphasizes experiential learning:

- **Build Real Projects**: Students create functional e-commerce platforms
- **Financial Integration**: Implement payment gateways and transaction systems
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Generate financial insights from e-commerce data
- **Business Case Studies**: Analyze successful Pakistani and international e-commerce ventures
- **Team Collaboration**: Work in groups mirroring real business environments

## Career Impact

Finance graduates with e-commerce expertise are increasingly valuable across multiple sectors:

- **FinTech Companies**: Understanding digital payments and online transactions
- **Retail & E-Commerce**: Managing financial operations for online businesses
- **Banking**: Supporting merchant services and digital banking initiatives
- **Consulting**: Advising clients on e-commerce financial strategies
- **Entrepreneurship**: Launching their own digital ventures
