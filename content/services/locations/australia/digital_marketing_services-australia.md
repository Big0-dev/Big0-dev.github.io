### How do you handle ACCC compliance in digital advertising?

Comprehensive ACCC compliance for Australian digital marketing:
**Price Display Compliance**:
- All consumer prices include GST (B2B may show ex-GST with clear indication)
- "Was/Now" pricing must reflect genuine previous selling price (substantiation required)
- Comparison pricing must be genuine and recent (typically within 3 months)
- No drip pricing—all mandatory fees shown before checkout

**Misleading Advertising Prevention**:
- All claims substantiated with evidence (performance claims, testimonials, statistics)
- No fine print contradicting headline claims
- Clear disclosure of material information affecting purchase decision
- Accurate product descriptions (especially for imported goods)

**Terms & Conditions**:
- Clear cancellation and refund policies
- Subscription terms clearly disclosed (renewal frequency, cancellation process)
- Free trial conditions (when trial ends, how to cancel, what happens if not cancelled)
- Delivery timeframes and costs disclosed before purchase

**Online Reviews & Testimonials**:
- No fake reviews (ACCC takes enforcement action)
- Disclosure if incentivized reviews (free product in exchange)
- No selective publication to mislead (cherry-picking only 5-star reviews)
- Verified purchaser indication where possible

**Country of Origin**:
- Clear disclosure for imported products
