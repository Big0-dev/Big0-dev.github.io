- **WooCommerce** (25%): Ideal if you already have WordPress, need complete customization control, or have a developer relationship. More flexibility but requires more technical maintenance.

### How do you ensure GST compliance for Australian e-commerce?

Comprehensive GST compliance built into every Australian e-commerce project:
- **Automatic GST Calculation**: 10% GST automatically added to taxable products, with exemptions for GST-free categories (basic food, some healthcare, education materials)
- **Price Display**: Consumer-facing prices include GST (legally required in Australia). Business customers can toggle to ex-GST view if B2B functionality enabled.
- **Accounting Integration**: Direct integration with Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks for automated GST reporting. Transaction data flows to accounting software for BAS preparation.
- **International Sales**: No GST on exports to international customers (tax exemption logic based on shipping address).
- **Reporting**: Detailed GST reports showing total GST collected, GST by product category, and GST credits on business expenses.
- **Low-Value Imported Goods**: For businesses importing and selling, we track the $1,000 threshold for GST on low-value imported goods.

All implementations reviewed by Australian e-commerce accountants to ensure ATO compliance.
