### Can automation handle Australian compliance requirements like BAS and STP?

Absolutely. We've built extensive automation for Australian tax compliance including Business Activity Statement (BAS) preparation, Single Touch Payroll (STP) Phase 2 reporting, and other ATO obligations. Our BAS automation extracts data from accounting systems, calculates G1-G24 labels according to ATO rules, validates data quality, and can integrate with Standard Business Reporting (SBR) software for electronic lodgment. For STP, we automate payroll event reporting with correct disaggregation, country codes, and employment basis as required by Phase 2. We also handle TPAR (Taxable Payments Annual Report) for businesses paying contractors, FBT calculations, and PAYG installments.
