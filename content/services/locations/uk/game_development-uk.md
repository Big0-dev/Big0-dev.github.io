## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can we benefit from Video Games Tax Relief (VGTR) in the UK?
VGTR provides substantial tax relief for UK game development, offering 20% additional deduction on qualifying expenditure. This translates to 16.2%-25% effective tax relief depending on your company's profitability. To qualify, your game must pass the BFI cultural test (achieving 16+ points through British cultural content, UK development team, UK locations, etc.). We handle the entire VGTR process: integrating qualifying British elements into your game design, preparing the BFI cultural test application, identifying qualifying costs (UK salaries, UK contractors, UK expenses), submitting interim and final HMRC claims, and maximizing relief alongside R&D tax credits. Our clients typically receive £150,000-£250,000 tax relief per £1M qualifying expenditure.

### What makes the UK's Guildford game development cluster special?
Guildford is known as the UK's game development capital with a legendary heritage. It's home to developers from Lionhead (Fable), Bullfrog (Theme Park, Dungeon Keeper), Media Molecule (LittleBigPlanet), and many other iconic UK studios. This creates a rich talent pool of experienced game developers who've shipped AAA titles and innovative indie games. Guildford offers proximity to London's publishers and investors while maintaining lower costs, access to University of Surrey's games programs for graduate recruitment, and a collaborative games community. Many consider it the perfect balance of talent, cost, and culture for UK game development.

### Do you handle PEGI rating for UK game releases?
Yes, we manage the complete PEGI (Pan European Game Information) rating process required for UK game distribution. Our services include: comprehensive game content review identifying descriptors (violence, bad language, fear, gambling, etc.), PEGI questionnaire completion with accurate content reporting, age rating application and liaison with PEGI administrators, appeals process if initial rating is contested, and PEGI OK certification for all-ages games. We also handle BBFC (British Board of Film Classification) requirements for physical releases. PEGI certification typically costs £1,000-£4,000 and takes 2-4 weeks. Proper PEGI compliance is essential for UK retail distribution and platform approval.

### Can you create games featuring UK football (soccer) clubs and leagues?
Yes, but licensing is complex and expensive. For official Premier League, Championship, or club content, you need licenses from: the Premier League/EFL (league rights), individual clubs (logos, names, colors), FIFPro (player names and likenesses), and stadium owners (for realistic stadium recreation). License costs range from £50,000-£500,000+ annually depending on scope. Alternatively, we can create football games with: fictional clubs and players, generic leagues inspired by British football, unlicensed real clubs (different names/logos but recognizable), or grassroots/non-league football (often easier licensing). We have experience negotiating UK football licenses and creating compelling unlicensed football games.
