Building privacy considerations into every stage of app development. We minimize data collection, implement purpose limitation, ensure data accuracy, and enable data portability as required by GDPR and DPA 2018.

### ICO Mobile Privacy Guidance

Following specific ICO guidance for mobile apps, including transparent privacy notices accessible within apps, lawful basis for processing, and special considerations for children's apps (Age Appropriate Design Code).

### User Consent Management

Implementing granular consent mechanisms that give users control over data processing. We create clear, understandable consent requests, honor user preferences, and maintain detailed consent records.

End-to-end encryption for sensitive data, secure data transmission (TLS 1.3), encrypted local storage, and secure authentication. Regular security audits and penetration testing ensure ongoing protection.

### How long does mobile app development take in the UK?

Mobile app development timelines vary based on complexity. A simple MVP can launch in 8-12 weeks, mid-complexity apps typically require 12-20 weeks, and enterprise applications may need 20-40 weeks or more. ### Should I build native iOS/Android apps or use cross-platform frameworks?

The decision depends on your budget, timeline, performance requirements, and feature needs. Native development (Swift for iOS, Kotlin for Android) offers best performance, full access to platform features, and optimal user experience, but costs more due to separate codebases. Cross-platform frameworks (React Native, Flutter) share 70-90% of code between platforms, reducing costs by 30-50% and accelerating time-to-market, with near-native performance suitable for most apps. We analyze your specific requirements and recommend the optimal approach for your UK business.
