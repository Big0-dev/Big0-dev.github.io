## Frequently Asked Questions

### Should I build native iOS/Android apps or use cross-platform development?
The choice depends on your specific needs, budget, and timeline. Native development (separate Swift and Kotlin codebases) offers maximum performance, access to latest platform features, and the most polished user experience—ideal for consumer apps competing in crowded US markets or apps requiring complex platform integrations. Cross-platform (React Native or Flutter) reduces costs by 30-40% and accelerates time-to-market, making it excellent for MVPs, B2B apps, or startups validating product-market fit. We'll analyze your specific requirements, target audience, and business goals to recommend the optimal approach for your US market entry.

### How long does it take to develop and launch a mobile app in the USA?
Timeline varies based on complexity and features. A simple MVP with basic functionality typically takes 12-16 weeks from concept to App Store launch, including design, development, testing, and submission. Medium complexity apps with custom features, API integrations, and payment processing require 20-26 weeks. Complex apps with advanced features like real-time sync, video streaming, or AR capabilities may need 30-40 weeks. We can accelerate timelines for startups with funding deadlines or market launch targets through dedicated team allocation and streamlined development sprints.

### What compliance requirements apply to mobile apps in the USA?
US mobile apps face various compliance requirements depending on their functionality and target audience. COPPA applies to apps directed at children under 13, requiring parental consent and restricted data collection. CCPA/CPRA affects apps collecting data from California residents (and increasingly other states), requiring privacy disclosures and user rights to data deletion. Healthcare apps handling protected health information must comply with HIPAA, including encryption, access controls, and Business Associate Agreements. Financial apps face various regulations depending on services offered. We have deep expertise in all these areas, ensuring your app launches compliant and stays compliant as regulations evolve.
