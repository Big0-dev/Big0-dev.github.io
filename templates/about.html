Our Story

How We Got Here

Big0 started with a question: what would happen if an engineering lab operated like a product company?

Founded in 2025, Big0 exists because we saw too many companies burning money on AI consulting that never shipped. We take the opposite approach: build first, talk about it later. Every service we offer has evidence behind it — a shipped product, a published paper, or a production deployment.

Today we serve clients across the UK, US, and Pakistan — from law firms deploying document AI to non-profits building membership dashboards. We're small, deliberate, and we ship.
