Our Story

Big0 was born from a shared vision: to make cutting-edge AI accessible and actionable for businesses of all sizes while prioritizing ethical frameworks.

Our founders - Zahida, Hassan, and Rooshaan - recognized that while AI was transforming industries, most solutions lacked both practical business applications and ethical safeguards. They saw an opportunity to bridge this gap.

Starting in 2023 with just five team members, we've grown into an international AI partner serving clients across four continents. Our commitment remains unchanged: deliver transformative technology that drives results while upholding our core values.
