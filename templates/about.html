Our Story

How We Got Here

Big0 exists because we've been on the other side of bad outsourcing.

We've seen startups burn through their seed round on agencies that delivered slide decks instead of software. We've watched companies hire "AI consultants" who couldn't ship a working prototype. We started Big0 to be the team we wished existed when we needed one.

We're a small team based in Islamabad. The CEO writes code. The CTO reviews every PR. When you hire us, you get direct access to senior engineers who make technical decisions — not project managers who relay messages. We show working software every two weeks, not status reports.

Today we build AI-powered applications, custom software, and full-stack products for startups and growing companies across the UK, US, and Pakistan. We're small, deliberate, and we ship.
